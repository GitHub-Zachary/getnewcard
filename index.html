Business Card Design Guide

Essential Elements

Every professional business card should include these key elements:

  • Full Name - Your complete name in a readable font
  • Job Title - Your position or professional role
  • Company Name - Business or organization name
  • Contact Information - Phone, email, website
  • Logo or Branding - Visual identity element

Keep the design clean and avoid cluttering your card with too much information. Remember, the goal is to make it easy for people to contact you.

Design Best Practices

Follow these professional design principles for maximum impact:

  • Use Readable Fonts - Avoid overly decorative typefaces
  • Maintain Hierarchy - Make your name most prominent
  • Choose Colors Wisely - Stick to 2-3 colors maximum
  • Include White Space - Don't fill every inch of the card
  • Consider Print Quality - Use high-resolution elements

A well-designed business card reflects your professionalism and attention to detail, making a lasting first impression.
